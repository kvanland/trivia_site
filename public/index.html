Hosting

  1. You'll be given a 5 digit game code at the top of the page. Share it with everyone who wants to play!

    Example:

    Game Code: 12345

  2. Once players have joined you'll see them in the table with their name, score, buzz-in order, and buttons to increase or decrease their scores.

    Example:
    Name Score Buzz # Correct?
    Piplup 9 1

  3. You'll have 4 buttons on the bottom of the screen. The first is to reveal the question to the people are playing. The second is to reveal the answer to the question. The third is to request a new question from the game. The fourth and last button resets the buzz ins from the players.
